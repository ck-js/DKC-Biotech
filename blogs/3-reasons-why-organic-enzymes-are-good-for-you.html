3 Reasons Why Organic Enzyme Products are Good For You & Your Pets

Published on November 29, 2023

When it comes to our pets and the environment we share with them like our living room, kitchen or even the bedroom its important that we maintain a clean and safe environment for ourselves as well as the comfort and wellbeing of your precious pets.

With so many products on the market it can be difficult to decide and choose which products to use and what does it mean if a product is organic or enzyme based and how do they work compared to products that are not organic or enzyme based?

Lets break it down and dive deeper into the 3 reasons why we should use organic enzyme based odor eliminator products for the health of ourselves and our pets.

Are Enzymes effective at eliminating odors ?

Effective odor elimination: Enzymes are highly effective in breaking down organic waste and eliminating odors [1]. They work by breaking down the molecules that cause the odor, resulting in a more thorough and long-lasting elimination.

Made with the environment in mind

Environmentally friendly: Organic enzyme-based odor eliminators are derived from natural sources and are biodegradable. They do not contain harsh chemicals or synthetic fragrances, making them safer for the environment [2].

Are enzyme odour elimnators safe ?

Safe for pets and humans: Enzyme-based odor eliminators are generally safe to use around pets and humans. They do not release harmful fumes or residues that can be harmful to health [3].

Where can we use Enzyme products

Versatile: Enzyme-based odor eliminators can be used on a variety of surfaces, including carpets, upholstery, fabrics, and hard surfaces. They are suitable for eliminating odors caused by pet accidents, food spills, and other organic sources [4].
